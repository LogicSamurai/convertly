Frequently Asked Questions

Is Convertly really free? Yes! Convertly is completely free with no registration required. There are no file size limits, no conversion limits, and no hidden fees. It's powered by open-source Pandoc and runs on free infrastructure.

What formats can I convert? Convertly supports 13+ formats including Markdown, HTML, DOCX, PDF, ODT, RST, LaTeX, plain text, MediaWiki, EPUB, JSON, Org-mode, AsciiDoc, and CSV. Convert between any two formats seamlessly.

Are my files safe? Absolutely. All files are processed in memory or stored temporarily in /tmp, and are deleted within 2 seconds of download. We keep no logs, no records, and no copies of your documents.

How does Convertly work? Convertly uses Pandoc, the gold-standard document converter used by academics and professionals worldwide. Our Go backend runs Pandoc in a secure containerized environment with strict timeouts and resource limits.

Supported Formats

Convertly supports 27+ document formats, powered by Pandoc's comprehensive conversion engine. Whether you need to convert Markdown to HTML, PDF to Word, Excel to CSV, PowerPoint to HTML, or any other combination, Convertly handles it all instantly.
